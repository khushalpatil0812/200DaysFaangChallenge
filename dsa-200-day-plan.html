THE PRO PLAYBOOK
How to use this plan like an engineer, not a student. The habits that separate crackers from followers.
⚡ EXECUTION RULES
🧠
Always attempt first. No solution for 30 min minimum. Pain = learning. Google is a last resort. Your brain rewires when it struggles.
🗣️
Talk out loud. Every problem — narrate your thought process. "I'm thinking of using a hashmap because..." Silence in interviews kills.
📝
Maintain an error log. Every wrong solution goes in a Notion doc. Pattern: mistake → root cause → correct approach → key insight.
⏱️
Time yourself. Easy = 15 min. Medium = 25 min. Hard = 40 min. If you're stuck past that, look at the approach hint, not the solution.
🔁
Spaced repetition. Week 1 problems → review in Week 3. Week 3 → review in Week 6. Use Anki cards for patterns, not solutions.
💪
Quality > quantity. 3 problems deeply understood beats 10 solutions copy-pasted. Know WHY your complexity is O(n log n) not just that it is.
📐
Master templates. Sliding window, BFS/DFS, backtracking, DP state transition, two-pointer — know them cold. Derive, don't memorize answers.
🏗️
SD framework URFCDE: Understand → Requirements → Frontend/API → Capacity → Data model → Execution. Use it every single SD session.
🎯
Resources: NeetCode 150, Blind 75, Grokking SD interview, ByteByteGo, AlgoExpert (optional), Pramp for mocks.
